Where It Matters

Two Markets. One Platform.

KADI Optics addresses two distinct but urgent unmet needs — with the same core optical sensing platform adapted for each setting.

🏥

Clinical — Cardiac Surgery & ECMO

Hospital / Perioperative Setting

During CPB and ECMO, patients receive high-dose heparin. Coagulation must be monitored continuously — but current tests (ACT, TEG) require blood samples and leave monitoring blind spots of 10–15 minutes. KADI places a fiber probe directly on the circuit for continuous live data.

  • Eliminates monitoring gaps during cardiac surgery
  • ECMO-compatible — designed for long-duration monitoring
  • Directly informs heparin dosing in real time
  • Cardiac anesthesiologists and perfusionists as primary users
🏠

Home — DOAC & Warfarin Patients

Remote Patient Monitoring

Over 6 million Americans take anticoagulants daily. Warfarin patients require frequent INR testing — painful and time-consuming. DOAC patients have no home monitoring option at all. The KADI wrist device would be the first continuous, non-invasive coagulation monitor for home use.

  • First DOAC home monitor — a unique, unmet capability
  • Replaces daily or weekly finger-stick blood tests
  • Streams live data to smartphone for patient and physician
  • RPM reimbursement eligible (CPT 99454 / 99457)
